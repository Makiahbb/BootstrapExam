Wii

The Wii was also a popular Nintendo gaming system. Since it has a different gameplay than the other systems. The controller was fit to allow an “interactive” gaming experience to go along with the sensor. The controller was a stick rectangle with a wristband. Just as the NES, the Wii also had popular groundbreaking games. The graphics, originality and gameplay made (most) the games enjoyable to be played as multiplayer games. These factors in total contributed to their popularity. They later came up with the Wii U.
